Treemap Diagram Demo

This is a demo of the new treemap diagram type in Mermaid.

Basic Treemap Example

treemap
    "Root"
        "Branch 1"
            "Leaf 1.1": 10
            "Leaf 1.2": 15
        "Branch 2"
            "Branch 2.1"
                "Leaf 2.1.1": 20
                "Leaf 2.1.2": 25
            "Leaf 2.2": 25
            "Leaf 2.3": 30
